Resolver & Routing Stack Settings

🤝 Handshake (HNS) Protocol Support

When enabled, `.hns` TLDs and registered Handshake root names will be securely resolved via your local `hnsd` daemon instance over 127.0.0.1:5301.

🧅 Tor (.onion) Network Support

When Tor is enabled, `.onion` addresses automatically route through the local SOCKS5 Tor proxy. Enabling "Route All Traffic" routes 100% of WebKit navigation requests through Tor circuits.
