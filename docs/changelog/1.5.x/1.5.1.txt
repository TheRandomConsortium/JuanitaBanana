Juanita Banana v1.5.1 Changelog
================================

Changes:
- Added a new Warn log level weight (1) to the LogLevel enum and environment variable parser in debug utilities.
- Switched newly introduced and existing raw console outputs (println!) for domain banning in gui.rs and message_handler.rs to structured log! macro calls at the Warn level.
- Migrated HTML viewer disk-saving console feedback to log! calls under the Info and Error levels.
